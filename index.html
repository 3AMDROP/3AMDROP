Refund Policy

Clear terms for returns, exchanges, and order issues.

Returns Window

Customers can request a return within 7 days of delivery if the item is unworn, unwashed, and in original condition.

Non-Returnable Items

Items returned with visible wear, damage caused after delivery, or missing original packaging may be refused.

Damaged Or Wrong Orders

If a customer receives the wrong item or a damaged product, they should contact 3AM Worldwide within 48 hours of delivery.

Refund Timing

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Bank timing can vary depending on the payment provider.

To request a return or report an order issue, contact us through the form above and include your order number, full name, and reason for the request.
